Thousands of people have been forgotten in history because of who they loved or how they identified. Many of these LGBTQ+ figures made important progress for both their community and the world. Today, activists are still fighting to make the world a more accepting and safe space for future people to learn and grow in. Learning about different identities is vital to growth in all communities. It is important to shed light on what so many people have done to achieve and expand LGBTQ+ rights for millions of people around the world.
